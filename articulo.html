What Is Articulo

Articulo is a mobile app that provids a way to consume content that you trust. Follow people who inspire them and read what they read!

This project was a part of my Summer Internship at AOL, Inc (now Oath). As a team of 3 developers, we built the proof of concept prototype in 6 weeks and have a working iOS app in TestFlight.

I took a significant part in the implementation of the app in React Native, managing the code on BitBucket, creating weekly sprints, as well as the ideation for the app concept and product development.
